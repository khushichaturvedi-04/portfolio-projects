## Overview

Designed and flight-simulated a high-power rocket in OpenRocket for a NAR/Tripoli Level 1 high-power certification flight, then flew it successfully to earn certification.

## Design Highlights

- 4-inch diameter, single-stage airframe with an ogive polystyrene nose cone and three freeform plywood fins
- Powered by an AeroTech I140W motor (I-class, the minimum required for Level 1 certification), with a 10-second ejection delay tuned to the simulated coast time
- Single 42-inch ripstop nylon main parachute, deployed via motor ejection charge
- Full mass, center-of-gravity, and stability analysis performed in OpenRocket prior to flight

## Result

Successfully certified NAR/Tripoli Level 1 in high-power rocketry.
